Kickstarter Project

The Oculus Rift™ was a Kickstarter crowdfunding campaign that was successfully funded toward its goal of $250,000 within just 4 hours of launching. In just 36 hours they had surpassed $1,000,000 finally settling on $2,437,429, leaving the project 974% funded.

The campaign was launched by Palmer Luckey, a technology and virtual reality enthusiast who wanted a VR solution that was both more effective than what is currently on the market and inexpensive for gamers.

Check out the Oculus Rift Kickstarter page here: Oculus Rift: Step Into the Game.

Current Status

Currently development units are being manufactured and distributed to software developers and early adopters all across the world. The development kits cost $300 and include 4 months of Unity Pro.
