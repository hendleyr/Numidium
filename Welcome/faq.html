Frequently Asked Questions

We've tried to address any issues that you may run into while configuring your Oculus Rift™ for the best experience possible.

If there is something that we didn't cover, send us an email.

Which operating systems can I use?

The application should work for any operating system with a WebGL-enabled web browser, specifically Google Chrome.

Which web browsers can I use?

At the time of development, Google Chrome is the only we browser that provides reliable results for all components of the scene.

Google Chrome can be downloaded for free here.

Why won't my movement work?

Hardware Configuration

Ensure that your Oculus Rift™ HMD is:

  • Plugged into a power supply and powered on.
  • Connected to your PC via HDMI or DVI.
  • Connected to your PC via a USB cord.

Software Configuration

In order for your Oculus Rift™ HMD to communicate your head movements to your PC you will need additional software. We recommend oculus-rest, which can be downloaded for free here.

For more information about setting up oculus-rest, see How To Install Oculus-Rest.
